Platform Architecture

DATA SOURCE

Data Sources

DeltaMax connects to a wide variety of data sources including relational databases, data warehouses, data lakes, enterprise applications, flat files, and REST APIs. Our platform supports both cloud-native and on-premise data sources, ensuring seamless integration with your existing infrastructure. We provide native connectors for popular databases like PostgreSQL, MySQL, Oracle, and SQL Server, as well as cloud platforms including AWS, Azure, and GCP. The platform automatically discovers and catalogs available data assets, creating a comprehensive inventory of your data landscape. With support for structured, semi-structured, and unstructured data, DeltaMax ensures no data source is left unmonitored. This flexible connectivity layer forms the foundation for comprehensive end-to-end data quality monitoring across your entire organization.

1

INGESTION LAYER

Ingestion Layer

The ingestion layer handles both batch and streaming data ingestion, processing millions of records per minute with low latency. Automatic schema inference and validation ensure that incoming data structures are properly understood and any schema changes are detected immediately. The platform performs initial data profiling during ingestion, calculating basic statistics like row counts, null percentages, unique values, and data type distributions. For streaming data, we support real-time ingestion through technologies like Apache Kafka, AWS Kinesis, and Google Pub/Sub, enabling instant quality validation. Incremental ingestion capabilities minimize resource usage by processing only changed data, while full refresh options are available for historical analysis. This layer ensures that data is reliably captured and prepared for quality analysis before it enters your data pipelines.

2

PROCESSING & ANALYSIS

Processing & Analysis

The processing engine executes comprehensive data quality checks including completeness,uniqueness, consistency, accuracy, and validity validations against your datasets. Machine learning algorithms detect anomalous patterns and outliers, automatically learning the normal behavior of your data over time. Statistical drift detection using Population Stability Index (PSI) and Kolmogorov-Smirnov (KS) tests continuously monitor for data distribution changes. A flexible business rule engine allows you to define custom validation rules using a simple DSL, enabling domain-specific quality requirements. The engine performs both rule-based and ML-based validation in parallel, providing a holistic view of data health. All processing is distributed and scalable, handling petabytes of data across thousands of tables without performance degradation.

3

TRUST & SCORE ENGINE

Trust & Scoring

The scoring engine computes five key metrics: Anomaly Score, Drift Score, Business Score, Freshness Score, and the overall Trust Score, providing a single source of truth for data reliability. Each score is calculated using weighted algorithms that consider historical patterns, rule violations, and data timeliness. Anomaly Scores flag unusual data points that deviate from expected statistical distributions, while Drift Scores quantify how much dataset characteristics have changed over time. Business Scores measure adherence to your specific data quality rules and compliance requirements, customizable for different business domains. Freshness Scores monitor data latency and update frequency, ensuring your analytics are based on current information. The final Trust Score is a comprehensive 0-100 metric that gives stakeholders an instant understanding of data quality health across your entire data ecosystem.

4

STORAGE LAYER

Storage Layer

he storage layer provides persistent, optimized storage for metadata, quality results, and data lineage information. The Metadata Store maintains comprehensive information about your data assets including schemas, relationships, statistics, and historical snapshots. The Results Store preserves all quality check results, scores, and anomalies for historical trending and compliance auditing, with configurable retention policies. The Lineage Store tracks data movement and transformation paths across your pipelines, providing full visibility into data provenance and downstream dependencies. This layered storage approach ensures that your quality monitoring data is always available for analysis, reporting, and compliance verification.

5

VISUALIZE & ALERTS

Visualize & Alerts

The visualization layer provides interactive dashboards with real-time data quality metrics, trend analysis, and drill-down capabilities for detailed investigation. Automated reports can be scheduled and distributed to stakeholders, providing regular updates on data health across departments. Intelligent alerting systems notify data engineers and business users of quality issues.

Dataflow Overview

01 Ingest Data
02 Profile & Monitor
03 Analyze & Detect
04 Score & Validate
05 Visualize & Alert

Ingest Data

The foundation of the DeltaMax workflow begins with the Ingestion Layer, designed to handle both batch and streaming data at scale. This stage connects to a wide variety of sources, including databases, data warehouses, data lakes, and applications . The platform performs automatic schema inference and data profiling during ingestion, calculating key statistics like row counts, null percentages, and data type distributions . It supports incremental processing to minimize resource usage and is built to handle petabyte-scale environments like Google Cloud's BigQuery

Profile & Monitor

After ingestion, the Processing & Analysis Engine immediately begins profiling the data to establish a baseline of normal behavior—its unique "data rhythm" . This involves monitoring for completeness, uniqueness, consistency, and accuracy. By creating this baseline, the platform can differentiate between routine fluctuations and true anomalies, which significantly reduces alert fatigue . This continuous monitoring ensures that any deviation from the established pattern is automatically flagged for further analysis

Analyze & Detect

This stage is where DeltaMax's AI-powered anomaly detection comes into play, going beyond traditional rule-based checks . Using a combination of statistical methods like the Interquartile Range (IQR) method and advanced machine learning algorithms like Isolation Forest, the platform automatically identifies hidden issues and outliers . It also performs drift detection using statistical methods like the Population Stability Index (PSI) and T-tests to identify significant shifts in data distributions that might not be apparent through simple rules . This proactive approach uncovers the "unknown unknowns" that typically cause major data quality issues

Score & Validate

At the heart of DeltaMax's decision-making is the Trust & Scoring Engine, which quantifies data reliability into an actionable metric . This engine combines all signals from prior stages—anomalies, drift metrics, business rule violations, and data freshness—into a comprehensive, interpretable Trust Score (0-100) . It validates data against business rules and provides an "Agent Summary" that explains exactly what changed, where, and its potential impact, removing ambiguity and enabling faster, more confident decisions

\

Visualize & Alert

The final step transforms raw quality metrics into actionable intelligence through the Visualization & Alerts layer. The results are loaded into BigQuery and connected to dashboards (e.g., in Looker Studio) for real-time visualization of data health and trends . The platform's intelligent alerting system notifies engineers and stakeholders of critical issues via email, Slack, or webhooks, ensuring prompt resolution. Crucially, automated reason codes are generated to classify discrepancies, moving beyond simple difference counts to explain why records are different, drastically reducing investigation time
